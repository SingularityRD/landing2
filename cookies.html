Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Singularity uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and platform.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and give website owners information about how their site is used.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly (e.g., session management, security).
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings to enhance your experience.
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Session Cookies Maintain your session and authenticate your access Session
Authentication Tokens Keep you logged in and maintain security 7-30 days
Analytics (Google Analytics) Collect anonymous usage statistics 2 years
Preference Cookies Remember your language and region settings 1 year
Marketing Cookies Track campaign performance and deliver targeted ads 90 days

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party service providers to set cookies on our behalf for purposes such as analytics, advertising, and social media integration. These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or alert you to cookies. Check your browser's help section for instructions.
  • Opt-Out Links: Some third-party services provide opt-out mechanisms.
  • Do Not Track: Some browsers support Do Not Track signals, though we may not respond to them.

Note: Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website and services.

6. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our data practices. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
