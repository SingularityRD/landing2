Service Level Agreement

Last updated: March 17, 2025

Our commitment to service availability, performance, and support for all customers.

Service Commitment

Singularity is committed to providing high-availability, high-performance security services to our customers. This Service Level Agreement (SLA) outlines the service levels we guarantee and the remedies available if we fail to meet those levels.

Service Availability

We guarantee the following uptime percentages for our platform services, measured on a monthly basis:

Starter

99.0%

Monthly uptime guarantee

Growth

99.9%

Monthly uptime guarantee

Enterprise

99.99%

Monthly uptime guarantee with dedicated infrastructure

Service Credits

If we fail to meet the guaranteed uptime, we will provide service credits as follows:

  • 99.0% - 99.9%: 10% credit on monthly fees
  • 95.0% - 98.9%: 25% credit on monthly fees
  • 90.0% - 94.9%: 50% credit on monthly fees
  • < 90.0%: 100% credit on monthly fees

Credits will be applied to your next invoice. To request a credit, contact support@singularityrd.com within 30 days of the service disruption.

Exclusions

The SLA does not cover service interruptions caused by:

  • Force majeure events (acts of God, war, terrorism, etc.)
  • Actions or inactions of the customer or its agents
  • Customer's equipment, software, or network infrastructure
  • Scheduled maintenance (we will provide at least 48 hours notice)
  • Third-party service failures beyond our reasonable control

Scheduled Maintenance

We perform regular maintenance to ensure service quality. Maintenance windows are typically scheduled during off-peak hours (10 PM - 6 AM PST). We will provide advance notice of scheduled maintenance via:

  • Email notifications to account administrators
  • Status page updates at status.singularityrd.com
  • In-product notifications

Security Commitments

We are committed to maintaining the highest security standards:

  • All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Regular third-party security audits and penetration testing
  • SOC 2 Type II compliance
  • ISO 27001 certification
  • 24/7 security monitoring and incident response

Data Backup and Recovery

We maintain comprehensive backup and disaster recovery capabilities:

  • Automated daily backups of all customer data
  • Backups stored in geographically distributed locations
  • Point-in-time recovery capability for up to 30 days
  • Recovery Time Objective (RTO): 4 hours
  • Recovery Point Objective (RPO): 1 hour

Changes to SLA

We may update this SLA from time to time. Material changes will be communicated to customers with at least 30 days notice. Continued use of our services after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated SLA.
