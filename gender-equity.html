Our Initiatives

Comprehensive programs designed to foster inclusion and advance gender equity in cybersecurity.

🎓

Scholarship Program

Annual scholarships for women pursuing cybersecurity degrees, including mentorship and internship opportunities at Singularity.

🤝

Mentorship Network

Connecting women in cybersecurity with experienced leaders for career guidance, skill development, and networking opportunities.

💼

Return-to-Work Program

Supporting women re-entering the workforce after career breaks with training, flexible work arrangements, and skill refreshers.

🚀

Leadership Development

Specialized training programs to prepare women for leadership roles in cybersecurity and technology management.

🌍

Community Outreach

Partnerships with schools and organizations to inspire young women to pursue careers in cybersecurity and technology.
