🏢 About Singularity

Building the Future of Autonomous Security

We're a team of security researchers, AI engineers, and infrastructure experts united by a single mission: to protect the world's most critical systems from increasingly sophisticated threats.

Our Mission

To democratize enterprise-grade security through AI, making advanced protection accessible to organizations of all sizes.

🎯

Autonomy First

We believe security should work for you, not the other way around. Our AI-driven systems operate autonomously, reducing manual effort and human error.

🔬

Research-Driven

Our team actively discovers new vulnerabilities and attack vectors. We don't just react—we anticipate and prevent.

Global Impact

From IoT devices to satellite systems, we protect infrastructure that millions depend on. Security is a universal need.
